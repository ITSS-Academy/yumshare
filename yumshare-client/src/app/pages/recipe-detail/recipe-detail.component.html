Recipe Name

Spaghetti is one of the most beloved pasta dishes in the world, known for its long, thin strands and endless versatility. Whether paired with a rich tomato sauce, creamy Alfredo, or simply tossed with olive oil and garlic, spaghetti offers comfort and flavor in every bite. It’s a classic that can be enjoyed at family dinners, quick lunches, or festive gatherings alike.

Process Steps

1.Boil the pasta

Start by bringing about 1 liter of water to a boil in a pot, then add ½ teaspoon of salt. Once the water is boiling, drop in 80–100 g of spaghetti and cook it for 8–10 minutes, or accordi ng to the package instructions, until it reaches an al dente texture. When done, drain the pasta and set it aside, remembering to save 2–3 tablespoons of the cooking water for later use.
